## 🔥 Where to track concerts

**Global**

- [**Songkick**](https://www.songkick.com/) — follow artists, get show alerts.
- [**Eventbrite**](https://www.eventbrite.com/) — city-level listings; tune filters toward music.
- [**LiveNation**](https://www.livenation.com/) / [**Ticketmaster**](https://www.ticketmaster.com/) — major tours + official on-sales (always match the seller to your region).

**Ukraine-first**

- [**Concert.ua**](https://concert.ua/) — local listings hub for UA shows.
- [**Ticketclub**](https://ticketclub.com.ua/) — UA ticketing.

**Deep cuts**

- [**Setlist.fm**](https://www.setlist.fm/) — recent setlists when you want a realistic live set, not the playlist fantasy.
